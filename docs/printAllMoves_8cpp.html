Calculate castling moves for a given king.

Checks castling conditions for each king for each side:

  • Has the king or rook moved?
  • Are the squares between the king and rook unoccupied?
  • Is the king in check?
  • Would the king pass through or into check?
Postcondition
vector range is populated with castling moves.
Parameters
pieceFromTo determine side; white or black.
indexFromThe starting position of the king.
rangeThe vector to populate with legal moves.
boardThe chess board.
